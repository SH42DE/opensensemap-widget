openSenseMap Widget for including into your website

How to?

Find out your senseBox Id (from your url on opensensemap.org) and paste this HTML to your page.

<div data-sensebox-id="your-senseBox-Id" style="width:400px;height:600px;"></div> <script src="https://sensebox.de/opensensemap-widget/script.js"></script>
